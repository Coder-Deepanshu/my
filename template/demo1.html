Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about EduCard

How quickly can we implement EduCard at our institution?
Implementation typically takes 2-4 weeks depending on the size of your institution and your specific requirements. Our team will work with you to create a customized implementation plan.
What kind of training do you provide?
We provide comprehensive training for administrators, faculty, and students. This includes on-site sessions, video tutorials, documentation, and ongoing support through our help center.
Can EduCard integrate with our existing systems?
Yes, EduCard offers API integrations with many popular educational and administrative systems. Our technical team can work with you to ensure seamless integration with your current infrastructure.
What security measures are in place to protect our data?
We take security seriously. EduCard uses industry-standard encryption, regular security audits, and complies with data protection regulations. All data is stored on secure servers with multiple backup systems.
How often do you release updates?
We release minor updates monthly and major feature updates quarterly. All updates are included in your subscription and are designed to be non-disruptive to your operations.
