About Edutrack

Transforming education through innovative management solutions that empower institutions to focus on what matters most - student success.

Our Story

Edutrack was founded in 2010 by a group of educators and technologists who recognized the growing need for comprehensive education management systems. Frustrated by the disjointed and outdated systems that plagued educational institutions, our founders set out to create a unified platform that would streamline operations and enhance the learning experience.

Today, Edutrack serves over 500 educational institutions worldwide, from small private schools to large university systems. Our platform has evolved to include cutting-edge features like AI-powered analytics, mobile accessibility, and seamless integration with existing systems.

We remain committed to our original mission: to empower educational institutions with technology that simplifies administration, enhances learning outcomes, and fosters collaboration between students, teachers, and administrators.

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that drive our innovation

Our Mission
